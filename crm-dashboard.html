CRM Dashboard: Sales Performance Analysis

An interactive Tableau dashboard to track pipeline metrics, benchmark team performance, and optimize the sales funnel.

Objective

To develop a comprehensive dashboard that provides sales leadership with a clear view of the sales pipeline, identifies bottlenecks, and highlights high-performing agents and products.

Tools Used

Tableau Salesforce SQL Excel

Data Source

Anonymized sales data exported from a Salesforce CRM instance.

The Live Dashboard

Supporting Visualizations

Data Integration & Analysis

The project started by connecting directly to Salesforce data via SQL to extract raw sales pipeline information. This data was then cleaned and structured to create a reliable foundation for analysis. I analyzed conversion rates at each stage of the sales funnel, benchmarked performance across different sales teams, and investigated the impact of pricing elasticity on deal closure rates.

Key Findings & Dashboard Features

The analysis revealed a significant bottleneck in the sales process, with a **49% drop-off rate** after the initial engagement stage. The interactive Tableau dashboard was designed to highlight these insights, featuring:

  • A dynamic sales funnel visualization to track deal progression.
  • Leaderboards to benchmark team and individual agent performance.
  • Filters for product sectors to identify top-performing product lines.

Recommendations & Business Impact

Based on the findings, I proposed several funnel optimization strategies, including targeted training for mid-funnel deal progression and a re-evaluation of the lead qualification process. The dashboard is now used by sales leadership to monitor team performance in real-time and make data-driven decisions to improve conversion rates.
